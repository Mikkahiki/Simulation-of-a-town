Project Purpose

This simulation models real climate leadership challenges. Players must balance emissions reduction, economic stability, and public approval while avoiding climate collapse.

The model combines environmental science, mathematics, and decision theory.

Scenario Analysis

Scenarios simulate real world climate decisions. Good choices reduce emissions but may slow economy. Neutral choices balance systems. Bad choices increase risk for short term gain.

System Interpretation

Rising temperature → insufficient emission control. Stable temperature → sustainable policy balance. Falling temperature → successful climate recovery.

Risk Evaluation
